Murat Onur Yildirim

Hi, I am a PhD candidate under the supervision of Joaquin Vanschoren at the department of Mathematics and Computer Science in TU Eindhoven.

My research focuses on continual learning, inspired by neuroscience and grounded in principles like sparsity and modularity.
