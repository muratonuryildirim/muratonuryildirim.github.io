Murat Onur Yildirim

Hi, I am a PhD candidate under the supervision of Joaquin Vanschoren and Decebal Constantin Mocanu at the department of Mathematics and Computer Science in TU Eindhoven.

I specialize in developing autonomous continual learners for computer vision operating under memory, computation, data, and label constraints.
